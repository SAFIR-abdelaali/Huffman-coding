Gun Park is one of the most feared and respected characters in Lookism.
Known as the "Legendary Gangster," Gun is the leader of the Workers' 1st Affiliate.
He is incredibly strong, with unmatched combat skills and a ruthless personality.
Gun is also a master strategist, often manipulating others to achieve his goals.
Despite his violent nature, he has a code of honor and respects those who prove their worth.
Gun's signature move is his devastating punch, capable of taking down any opponent.
He has trained many fighters, including Daniel Park, shaping them into powerful warriors.
Gun's past is shrouded in mystery, but his influence on the Lookism universe is undeniable.
Whether as an enemy or an ally, Gun Park remains a force to be reckoned with.
